About me

I'm a freelance UX UI designer and Web developer based in Morocco. I have a serious passion for all kinds of visual communication and new web and mobile technologies, but my main focus is in front-end technologies (such as ReactJS), while expanding my knowledge in testing, back-end development and APIs.

My Marketing and communication experience in the btob and btoc world is a key asset that helps me understand the real needs of my partners, and also provides them with a high end product that meets their expectations.

I also provide on-going support and maintenance services to ensure that their websites are running at their best performance.
